Chapter 10

Pasta Carbonara

Pasta Carbonara was one of Jeff’s favorite pasta dishes. This dish is a great way to use up any veggies still waiting for their turn.

Stats

20 Minutes

1 Serving

Ingredients

2 ounces dry pasta

4 ounces shrimp, peeled and deveined

1 teaspoon grapeseed oil

1 egg

1 tablespoon parmesan cheese, grated

1 cup mixed leftover veggies

1 teaspoon Italian seasoning

1/2 teaspoon crushed red peppers

1 tablespoon bacon bits

Method

Cook pasta according to package directions.

While the pasta cooks, beat the egg and parmesan cheese together.

Once the pasta is drained (reserve 1/2 cup of the starchy water) add the egg/cheese mixture to the hot pasta. Stir constantly for 3 minutes. This will prevent your eggs turning into scrambled eggs. Add the pasta water as needed, I added 1/4 cup to my sauce.

In a skillet, heat the grapeseed oil over medium heat.

Season the shrimp with salt and pepper. Cook for about 3 minutes total, depending on the size of your shrimp. Flip once. Set aside.

Add the bacon bits to the pan and cook for 1 minute. Add your veggies and sauté for 3 minutes. Add crushed red pepper for a bit of heat, which is optional.
