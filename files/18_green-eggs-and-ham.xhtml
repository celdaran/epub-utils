Green Eggs and Ham

This is such a simple recipe and so delicious. If you do not like spinach, use chopped broccoli. If you do not like chopped broccoli, then you’ll just have to find some actual green ham.

Stats

20 Minutes

1 Serving

Ingredients

1 ounce deli ham, chopped

1 cup baby spinach, chopped

1/2 cup liquid egg whites

One ounce pepper jack cheese

Method

Preheat oven to 350 degrees.

In the bottom of a 4-inch ramekin, layer 1 ounce of cut deli ham, 1 cup of baby spinach and 1/2 cup of egg whites.

Place a slice of pepper jack cheese on top.

Bake at 350 degrees for 20 minutes until the cheese is nice and melted, and the eggs are set.

Best served with Sam I Am.
