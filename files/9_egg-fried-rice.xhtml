Egg Fried Rice

This simple egg fried rice is not only super cheap, but ready in minutes for a quick lunch or weeknight meal.

Stats

14 minutes

1 serving

Ingredients

1 cup cooked rice, cold (leftovers work great for this!)

1 cup frozen peas and carrots, drained

1 large egg

1 tablespoon soy sauce

1 teaspoon rice wine vinegar

1 teaspoon sriracha

Method

Heat a skillet over medium heat. Spray with avocado oil spray.

Add frozen peas and carrots, and sauté for about 3-4 minutes.

Add rice, soy sauce, vinegar, sriracha.

Stir until rice is evenly coated.

Form a well in the middle of the pan and add the egg.

Cook for 1 to 2 minutes per side. Chop up and stir into the fried rice.
