Breakfast Casserole Muffins

Picture your comfort food breakfast casserole lightened up a bit, gluten-free and WW friendly! I realize this is not a recipe for one, but this freezes beautifully!

Stats

30 Minutes

10 Servings

Ingredients

10 ounces gluten-free skinny pizza dough (see page )

5 ounces turkey breakfast sausage (I used @jennieo)

2 tablespoons gluten-free flour

3/4 cup unsweetened almond milk

1 teaspoon crushed red pepper

1/2 teaspoon pepper

10 eggs

1-1/4 cups reduced fat mozzarella

1 teaspoon paprika

1 tablespoon fresh parsley, chopped

Method

Heat oven to 375 degrees.

Spray muffin tin with avocado oil spray. Place 1 ounce of dough into the bottom to make 10 muffins.

To make the sausage gravy: Cook turkey sausage in a skillet, breaking up the sausage as it cooks. Add 2 tablespoons gluten-free flour and cook for 1 minute. Stir in almond milk and cook a couple minutes until thickened. Stir in the pepper and crushed red pepper; set aside.

Add one scrambled egg to each muffin tin. Add a tablespoon of sausage gravy and top with 1/8 cup of cheese on each muffin. Sprinkle with paprika and bake for 19-20 minutes.

Let cool for 10 minutes before removing, and garnish with a tablespoon of gravy and chopped parsley.

Notes: These will last in the fridge for a few days. You can freeze on a cookie sheet individually for 30 minutes, then store in a Ziplock bag.
