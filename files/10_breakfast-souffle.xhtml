Breakfast Souffle

When I first made this recipe years ago, my mom made it immediately and it has been on her breakfast rotation ever since.

Stats

25 Minutes

1 Serving

Ingredient

1 ounce goat cheese (any flavor)

1 tablespoon fat free mayo

1 tablespoon skim milk

2 eggs

Cracked pepper

Dash of salt

Method

Mix all ingredients in a blender (I used a stick blender) until the cheese is incorporated into the eggs. Pour into a 5-ounce ramekin. I was worried when I saw it fill to the top, but it was fine. I did cook it on a cookie sheet just in case there was major spillage!

Bake at 425 degrees for 20 minutes, until the top starts to brown. It should be firm to the touch.

Note: If you make this as a meal prep, the soufflé will deflate, but will still taste delicious.
