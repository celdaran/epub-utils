Title

These air-fried “sous vide” egg bites require a silicone egg bite mold, but it will pay for itself since you will not have to go to the local coffee shop for the expensive ones!

Stats

16 Minutes

2 Servings

Ingredients

4 large eggs

1 ounce feta cheese

1/2 cup spinach

2 tablespoons real bacon bits

2 tablespoons half-and-half (or milk of choice)

1/2 teaspoon crushed red pepper

Salt and pepper

Method

Preheat air fryer for 5 minutes at 360 degrees.

In a blender, blend the eggs, feta, crushed red pepper, salt and pepper.

Chop up the spinach.

Spray silicone egg bites mold with avocado oil.

Divide the spinach and bacon bits between the cups. Pour in the egg mixture.

Bake for 11 minutes. Let cool 2 minutes before removing.

Serve with fresh fruit.

Note: This makes two servings, but the second serving will stay in the fridge for a few days. You’ll have a second quick breakfast later in the week.
