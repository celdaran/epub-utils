Chapter 16

Eggs in Purgatory

I first had this recipe in a restaurant that served it with homemade crusty bread – perfect for dipping into that delicious sauce!

Stats

20 Minutes

1 Serving

Ingredients

3/4 cup marinara sauce

2 large eggs

4 ounces red potato, cooked and diced

1 tablespoon part-skim ricotta cheese

1 ounce mozzarella cheese, shredded

Pinch of crushed red pepper

Salt and pepper, to taste

2 teaspoons fresh basil, chopped

Method

In a baking dish, lay the diced potatoes on the bottom.

Pour in the marinara sauce.

Crack two eggs on either side of the dish.

Dot the ricotta and mozzarella cheese around the dish.

Top with crushed red pepper, and salt and pepper to taste.

Bake at 425 degrees for 15 minutes - 10 minutes if you like a runny egg. If you are like me and do not like a runny egg at all, finish it off under the broiler for two minutes.

Optional: Serve with crusty bread for dipping.

I take that back, the crusty bread for dipping is required. *wink*
