The Perfect Hard-Boiled Egg

It took me the longest time to make perfect hard-boiled eggs. This is no fail – pinky swear!

Stats

10 Minutes

3 Servings

Ingredients

3 eggs (or however many you want)

Tap water (enough to cover the eggs by 1 inch)

Method

Carefully place the eggs in a pot.

Pour in tap water until eggs are covered to a depth of 1 inch.

Bring to a boil.

Once boiling, set a timer for 10 minutes.

About a minute before done, put water and a cup of ice in a bowl.

When the 10-minute buzzer goes off, transfer eggs to the cold-water bath and leave them alone. They must come back to room temperature.

Notes: Use right away or store in the refrigerator for up to one week.
