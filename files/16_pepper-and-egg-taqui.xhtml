Pepper and Egg Taquitos

These are perfect for a “Meatless Monday!” The key to getting crispy taquitos is to microwave the corn tortillas for 30-45 seconds before rolling them up. They will not split in your air fryer while baking, and avocado oil spray is all you need to get them crispy.

Stats

15 Minutes

1 Serving

Ingredients

3 corn tortillas (My favorite is El Milagro brand, but Mission extra thin corn tortillas work great too!)

2 tablespoons Alouette cheddar cheese spread

1/2 cup baby spinach, chopped

1/4 cup red pepper, diced

1/4 cup green pepper, diced

2 eggs, scrambled

1 tablespoon cilantro, chopped

Method

Preheat the air fryer to 360 degrees for five minutes.

In a skillet, sauté the peppers until soft, about 6-7 minutes.

Microwave the tortillas for 30-45 seconds – do not skip this step.

Divide the cheese spread between the tortillas, and add the eggs, peppers, and spinach to each taquito.

Roll up tightly. Spray with avocado oil spray and cook seam side down for 6 minutes at 360 degrees. Flip and cook an additional 4 minutes.

Serve with Greek yogurt and salsa for dipping. Garnish with cilantro.
